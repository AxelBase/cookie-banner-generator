Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Cookie Banner Text Generator, a client-side web utility designed to help you create compliant cookie banner text. We are committed to protecting your privacy and operate on a privacy-first principle. Since the tool runs entirely in your browser, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Cookie Banner Text Generator does not collect:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses.
  • Usage Data: Including IP addresses, browser type, device information, or location data.
  • Content Data: Any input you provide is processed locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Analytics Data: We do not use tracking tools or cookies to monitor user behavior.

3. How the Tool Works

When you use the Cookie Banner Text Generator:

  • Input and Processing: You input data directly into the browser interface. The tool generates cookie banner text locally using JavaScript.
  • No Data Transmission: No data is sent over the internet to us or any external service.
  • Temporary Local Storage: The browser may use temporary local storage for session data, but this is cleared upon reset or page reload.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services that collect data. The tool uses open-source libraries hosted locally or via CDNs, but these do not track users.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Cookie Banner Text Generator does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

We encourage users to practice good security habits, such as using secure browsers and devices.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tool is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. International Users

The tool is hosted in a jurisdiction compliant with international privacy standards, but since no data is collected, it adheres to regulations like GDPR and CCPA by default.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the effective date.
