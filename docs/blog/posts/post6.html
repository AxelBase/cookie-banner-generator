5 Common Cookie Banner Mistakes SMBs Must Avoid

Published: November 5, 2025

Cookie banners are a necessary evil, but getting them wrong can lead to non-compliance and frustrated customers. Here are 5 common mistakes SMBs make — and how our generator fixes them automatically.

Mistake #1: Lack of Transparency

Failing to clearly explain what data is being collected and why.

Mistake #2: Pre-Ticked Boxes

Assuming users have given consent when boxes are pre-ticked.

Mistake #3: No Opt-Out Option

Not providing a clear way for users to reject cookies.

Mistake #4: Overly Complex Language

Using legal jargon that confuses users.

Mistake #5: No Update Mechanism

Failing to provide a way for users to update their cookie preferences.

Key Takeaways

  • Be transparent about data collection.
  • Provide clear opt-in and opt-out options.
  • Use simple language.
  • Allow users to update preferences.
