The 30-Second Cookie Policy Every Shopify Store Should Use

The 30-Second Cookie Policy Every Shopify Store Should Use

Published: October 28, 2025

Shopify store owners often struggle with cookie policies. Most templates are too long, too legalistic, and too confusing for customers. The truth is, you don’t need a 20-page document to be compliant—you need a short, clear policy that explains cookies in plain English. That’s why we created a 30-second cookie policy: lawyer-reviewed, under 600 words, and ready to paste into your store.

Why Short Policies Work

Customers rarely read long cookie policies. A concise version respects their time, builds trust, and still meets compliance requirements. Regulators care about transparency, not word count.

What’s Inside

The 30-second policy covers the essentials: what cookies are, why they’re used, and how customers can opt out. It avoids jargon and focuses on customer benefits, like improved shopping experiences and personalized recommendations.

Key Features

  • Lawyer-reviewed for GDPR and CCPA compliance.
  • Written in plain English for accessibility.
  • Copy-paste ready for Shopify and other platforms.

FAQ

Is this policy enough for compliance?

Yes, for most SMBs. It covers the core requirements without unnecessary complexity.

Can I customize it?

Absolutely. Add details about your specific tools or analytics if needed.

Do customers actually read it?

Some do, but the point is clarity. Even skimmers will understand your intent quickly.

Compliance doesn’t have to be intimidating. A short, clear policy is often the best policy.
